01 / PROFILE

I am interested in that moment when an idea stops being a promise and begins to become a story, a team and a production plan.

I am currently in my fourth year of Audiovisual Communication at Universidad Católica del Uruguay. I expect to graduate in 2027, once my thesis is completed. I develop fiction projects from the initial idea and writing stages through screenwriting and production.

I am a software engineer. That background, together with my experience in product, support, automation and artificial intelligence, allows me to build processes and tools so that technology supports creativity rather than getting in its way.
